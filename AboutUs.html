About Us – Flixy Inc.

The home of innovation in smart entertainment technology.

Welcome to Flixy Inc. We are a team dedicated to transforming the way people watch TV by delivering simple, affordable, and powerful solutions. As the creators behind Flixy – America’s #1 Rated TV Smart Stick, our mission is to make unlimited entertainment accessible to everyone, without the burden of expensive monthly subscriptions.

With customers in thousands of homes worldwide, Flixy Inc. stands at the forefront of a new era in digital streaming freedom.
